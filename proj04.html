Tony

the front man

Tony is the founding member of Burnaround and the backbone of the band. He has many years of experience in the music scene and has dedicated many of those years to Burnaround.

Tony prefers to play his Gibson Les Paul or Firebird through a hand-made Rivera amp.

Dan

the bass

Dan’s soaring vocals contrast the bass guitar slung on his shoulder.

He plays a classic Fender Bass, with signs of wear that showing the time Dan has put into playing music.

Ryan

the guitar

Ryan's lead guitar playing adds a unique styling to the music Burnaround plays.

Ryan’s tone comes from Fender Telecasters through either an Orange or Blackstar amp (depending on his mood), along with a plethora of pedals in his signal chain.
